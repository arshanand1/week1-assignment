Text Formatting Tags

  1. Bold(b)
    • Used to bold the word or statement.
    • Should not be used for entire paragraph.
  2. Italic(i)
    • It is used to different between a word and a phrase
  3. Underline(u)
    • It is used to underline the word or sentence.
  4. Strong(strong)
    • Used to show the importance of some text.
